    Doctor Responsibilities
    Your Role:

    As a Doctor, you are responsible for conducting medical consultations, diagnosing patients, ordering lab tests, and prescribing medications.

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Patient Consultation: Conduct comprehensive medical consultations including:
      • Taking medical history (chief complaint, present illness, past history)
      • Performing physical examinations
      • Making clinical assessments and diagnoses
      • Developing treatment plans
    • Lab Test Orders: Order appropriate laboratory tests based on clinical findings
    • Prescriptions: Prescribe medications from the campaign formulary
    • Referrals: Make referrals to specialists when necessary
    • Follow-up: Provide follow-up instructions and care plans
    Consultation Workflow:
    1. Search for patients ready for consultation (vitals taken)
    2. Review patient demographics and vital signs
    3. Conduct consultation and document findings
    4. Order lab tests if needed
    5. Prescribe medications from formulary
    6. Complete consultation and provide follow-up instructions
    Quick Reference
    Consultation Components:
    History Taking:
    • Chief complaint
    • History of present illness
    • Past medical history
    • Family history
    • Social history

    Physical Examination:
    • General appearance
    • Systematic examination
    • Vital signs review

    Assessment & Plan:
    • Clinical assessment
    • Working diagnosis
    • Treatment plan
    • Lab orders
    • Prescriptions
    • Follow-up instructions

    Getting Started
    First Time Using the System?

    Follow these steps to conduct your first consultation:

    1. Click on "Find Patients" to search for patients
    2. Look for patients with status "Vitals Taken" - they're ready for consultation
    3. Click "Consult" next to a patient to start the consultation
    4. Fill in the consultation form with your findings
    5. Order lab tests if needed from the campaign's available tests
    6. Prescribe medications from the campaign formulary
    7. Save the consultation
    Tips for Efficient Consultations:
    • Review patient vitals before starting the consultation
    • Use the "Ready for Consultation" filter to find patients quickly
    • Lab tests are limited to those configured for each campaign
    • Prescriptions are limited to the campaign formulary
    • You can edit consultations until they're marked as completed
    • All consultations are automatically logged for audit purposes
