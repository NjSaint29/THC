    Vitals Clerk Responsibilities
    Your Role:

    As a Vitals Clerk, you are responsible for recording and updating patient vital signs and clinical parameters.

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Search Patients: Find patients by ID, name, or phone number
    • Record Vitals: Enter clinical parameters including:
      • Weight, Height, Temperature
      • Blood Pressure (Systolic/Diastolic)
      • Heart Rate
      • Blood Glucose levels
      • Women's health parameters (LMP, pregnancy status)
    • Update Records: Modify existing vital signs when needed
    • View Demographics: Access patient demographic information
    Workflow:
    1. Search for patient using Patient ID or name
    2. Verify patient identity and demographics
    3. Record or update vital signs
    4. Save the information to update patient status
    Quick Reference
    Normal Vital Sign Ranges:
    Blood Pressure:
    • Normal: <120/80 mmHg
    • Elevated: 120-129/<80
    • Stage 1: 130-139/80-89
    • Stage 2: ≥140/90

    Heart Rate:
    • Normal: 60-100 bpm

    Temperature:
    • Normal: 36.1-37.2°C

    BMI Categories:
    • Underweight: <18.5
    • Normal: 18.5-24.9
    • Overweight: 25-29.9
    • Obese: ≥30

    Blood Glucose:
    • Fasting: 70-100 mg/dL
    • Random: <140 mg/dL

    Getting Started
    First Time Using the System?

    Follow these steps to get started:

    1. Click on "Search Patients" to find a patient
    2. Use the Patient ID (format: TIKO-2025-XXXX) for fastest search
    3. Click "Record" next to a patient to enter their vitals
    4. Fill in the vital signs form and save
    Tips for Efficient Work:
    • Use the "Pending Vitals" view to see patients who need vitals recorded
    • The system automatically calculates BMI and categorizes blood pressure
    • You can update vitals multiple times if needed
    • All changes are automatically logged for audit purposes
