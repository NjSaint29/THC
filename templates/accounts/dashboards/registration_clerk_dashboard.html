    Registration Clerk Responsibilities
    Your Role:

    As a Registration Clerk, you are the first point of contact for patients and responsible for accurate patient registration and data entry.

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Patient Registration: Register new patients in the system
      • Collect complete demographic information
      • Verify patient identity and contact details
      • Obtain informed consent for campaign participation
      • Generate unique Patient IDs (TIKO-2025-XXXX format)
    • Data Management: Maintain accurate patient records
      • Update patient information as needed
      • Verify and correct data entry errors
      • Ensure data completeness and accuracy
    • Campaign Support: Assist with campaign operations
      • Understand campaign requirements and procedures
      • Guide patients through registration process
      • Coordinate with other staff members
    • Patient Communication: Provide excellent customer service
      • Explain campaign procedures to patients
      • Answer basic questions about the process
      • Ensure patient comfort and understanding
    Registration Workflow:
    1. Welcome patient and explain the campaign
    2. Collect demographic and contact information
    3. Verify patient identity and eligibility
    4. Obtain informed consent
    5. Complete registration and generate Patient ID
    6. Direct patient to next station (vitals)

    Getting Started
    First Time Using the System?

    Follow these steps to register your first patient:

    1. Click on "Register Patient" to start
    2. Select the appropriate active campaign
    3. Fill in all required patient information
    4. Ensure consent checkbox is checked
    5. Save the registration to generate Patient ID
    6. Write down or print the Patient ID for the patient
    Tips for Efficient Registration:
    • Have patients prepare their information in advance
    • Double-check spelling of names and contact details
    • Verify age matches date of birth if both provided
    • Ensure emergency contact information is complete
    • Explain the consent process clearly to patients
    • Keep Patient ID cards or slips ready for patients
    Important Notes:
    • Patient IDs are automatically generated in the format TIKO-2025-XXXX
    • Consent is required - patients cannot be registered without consent
    • All registrations are logged for audit purposes
    • Patient information can be updated after registration if needed
