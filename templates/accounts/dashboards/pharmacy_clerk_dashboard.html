    Pharmacy Clerk Responsibilities
    Your Role:

    As a Pharmacy Clerk, you are responsible for dispensing medications prescribed by doctors and maintaining accurate drug inventory records.

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Prescription Dispensing: Process and dispense medications
      • Review prescriptions for accuracy and completeness
      • Verify patient identity and prescription details
      • Dispense correct medications with proper labeling
      • Provide patient counseling on medication use
    • Inventory Management: Maintain drug stock levels
      • Monitor drug inventory levels
      • Track medication expiration dates
      • Record dispensing activities
      • Report stock shortages
    • Documentation: Maintain accurate records
      • Record all dispensing activities
      • Update patient medication histories
      • Track controlled substance usage
      • Generate dispensing reports
    • Patient Safety: Ensure safe medication practices
      • Check for drug allergies and interactions
      • Verify dosages and administration routes
      • Provide medication counseling
      • Report adverse drug reactions
    Dispensing Workflow:
    1. Receive prescription from doctor or patient
    2. Verify prescription details and patient identity
    3. Check drug availability and expiration dates
    4. Prepare and label medications accurately
    5. Counsel patient on proper medication use
    6. Record dispensing in system and update inventory
