    Campaign Manager Responsibilities
    Your Role:

    As a Campaign Manager, you are responsible for planning, creating, and overseeing health campaigns from inception to completion.

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Campaign Planning: Design and structure health campaigns
      • Define campaign objectives and scope
      • Set campaign dates and locations
      • Plan resource requirements
    • Campaign Configuration: Set up campaign parameters
      • Configure available lab tests
      • Set up drug formularies
      • Define consent text and procedures
      • Set patient capacity limits
    • Campaign Monitoring: Track campaign progress
      • Monitor patient registration numbers
      • Track consultation completion rates
      • Review lab test utilization
      • Analyze prescription patterns
    • Reporting: Generate campaign reports
      • Patient demographics analysis
      • Health outcomes tracking
      • Resource utilization reports
      • Campaign effectiveness metrics
    Campaign Lifecycle Management:
    1. Planning: Define campaign goals and requirements
    2. Setup: Configure lab tests, formulary, and parameters
    3. Launch: Activate campaign for patient registration
    4. Monitor: Track progress and adjust as needed
    5. Complete: Close campaign and generate final reports

    Campaign Management Guide
    Campaign Status Types:
    • Draft - Campaign being planned
    • Active - Campaign currently running
    • Completed - Campaign finished successfully
    • Cancelled - Campaign cancelled
    Best Practices:
    • Plan campaigns at least 2 weeks in advance
    • Configure all lab tests and drugs before activation
    • Monitor patient registration daily
    • Review resource utilization weekly
    Key Performance Indicators:
    • Patient Registration Rate: Patients per day
    • Consultation Completion: % of registered patients
    • Lab Test Utilization: Tests ordered vs. available
    • Prescription Rate: Medications dispensed
    • Campaign Efficiency: Patients per resource unit
    Reporting Schedule:
    • Daily: Patient registration numbers
    • Weekly: Progress and resource utilization
    • Monthly: Comprehensive campaign analysis
    • End of Campaign: Final outcomes report
