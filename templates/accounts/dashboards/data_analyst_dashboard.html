    Data Analyst Responsibilities
    Your Role:

    As a Data Analyst, you are responsible for analyzing health campaign data, generating insights, and creating reports for stakeholders.

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Data Analysis: Analyze campaign and patient data
      • Patient demographic analysis
      • Health outcomes assessment
      • Campaign performance metrics
      • Resource utilization analysis
    • Report Generation: Create comprehensive reports
      • Campaign summary reports
      • Patient demographic reports
      • Clinical outcomes analysis
      • Laboratory results summaries
    • Data Visualization: Present data in meaningful ways
      • Create charts and graphs
      • Design dashboards
      • Develop trend analyses
      • Build comparative studies
    • Quality Assurance: Ensure data integrity
      • Validate data accuracy
      • Identify data inconsistencies
      • Monitor data completeness
      • Recommend data improvements
    Analysis Categories:
    • Descriptive Analytics: What happened?
    • Diagnostic Analytics: Why did it happen?
    • Predictive Analytics: What might happen?
    • Patient Demographics: Age, gender, location
    • Health Outcomes: Diagnoses, treatments
    • Operational Metrics: Efficiency, throughput
    Key Metrics
    Campaign Performance:
    • Patient registration rate
    • Consultation completion rate
    • Lab test utilization
    • Prescription patterns
    • Follow-up compliance
    Patient Demographics:
    • Age distribution
    • Gender breakdown
    • Geographic distribution
    • Marital status patterns
    Clinical Outcomes:
    • Common diagnoses
    • Treatment effectiveness
    • Lab result patterns
    • Referral rates
