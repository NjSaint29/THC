Staff Registration Information

Join our healthcare team and make a difference!

Welcome to Tiko Health Campaign!

We're building a comprehensive healthcare management system to serve our community. As a staff member, you'll play a crucial role in delivering quality healthcare services.

How to Register

  1. Click the registration link provided by your administrator
  2. Fill out the registration form with your personal information
  3. Select your role based on your responsibilities
  4. Create secure login credentials (username and password)
  5. Submit the form to complete your registration
  6. Log in with your new credentials to access your dashboard

Available Staff Roles

Choose the role that matches your responsibilities in the health campaign:

1️⃣ Registration Clerk

Section A: Demographic and Biographic Data

  • Register new patients with auto-generated Patient IDs
  • Collect demographic information (name, age, sex, contact details)
  • Obtain patient consent for campaign participation
  • Cannot access clinical data, consultations, or lab results
2️⃣ Vitals Clerk

Section B: Clinical Parameters

  • Record patient vital signs (weight, temperature, blood pressure, heart rate)
  • Measure height, blood glucose, and pregnancy-related parameters
  • View patient demographics (read-only)
  • Cannot edit demographics or access consultations/lab results
3️⃣ Doctor

Sections C & E: Medical Consultation and Prescriptions

  • Conduct medical consultations and examinations
  • Make diagnoses and treatment plans
  • Order laboratory tests and write prescriptions
  • View full patient records including lab results
  • Cannot edit demographics or vital signs
4️⃣ Lab Technician

Section D: Laboratory Test Results

  • Process laboratory test orders from doctors
  • Enter test results with proper interpretation
  • Handle critical values with immediate notification
  • View patient demographics and vitals (read-only)
  • Cannot edit demographics, vitals, or consultations

Registration Requirements

Required Information:
  • Full name (first and last)
  • Valid email address
  • Unique username
  • Secure password
  • Role selection
  • Phone number (optional)
Password Requirements:
  • At least 8 characters long
  • Cannot be too common
  • Cannot be entirely numeric
  • Cannot be too similar to personal info

After Registration

What happens next?
  1. Immediate Access: You can log in immediately after registration
  2. Role-specific Dashboard: You'll see a dashboard tailored to your role
  3. Training Materials: Your dashboard includes guides and instructions
  4. Admin Oversight: Administrators can manage your account if needed
  5. Support: Contact your administrator for any questions or issues
